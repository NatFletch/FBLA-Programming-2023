How do I get points?

To get points you must attend school functions. Points are distributed by teachers at the event


How do I receive the item I purchased?

To receive the item you purchased just wait for a bit and eventually a teacher will deliver it to you. We are able to see all the invoices to efficiently hand out items


How do I make an account?

Sign up by clicking the register link on the log in page


Can this be used without an account?

Unfortunately not because this is how we track each student by name and how many points they have


How do I get higher on the leaderboard?

You can simply get higher on the leaderboard by earning more points


Where are all the events listed at?

You can find all of the events on the home page of the application


I'm a teacher, how do I get a teacher account?

Simply ask a school admin to promote your account to a teacher account


How much does each item cost?

T-Shirt: 10 points, Snack Voucher: 5 points, Pen: 2 points


What is this application for?

This application is to increase student involvement at Smithville High School by awarding points to those who attend school events. The points can then further be spent to win prizes or you can stock up on points to get high on the leaderboard.


Who made this application?
